The problem

You blurred the input.
Then it moved.

You hit record and start walking through the books. Your bank balance is on the dashboard, so you cover it. Then you navigate to Transactions. It's the opening balance. Then it's in the export filename. The blur stayed on the dashboard.

Fixed-region blurs don't follow data. Hushh does. The secret gets hidden wherever it surfaces, automatically.
