The problem

Then it moved.

Fixed-region blurs don't follow data. Hushh does. The secret gets hidden wherever it surfaces — automatically.

Three steps to invisible.

No signup. No typing. No copy-pasting. You point at what you want hidden. That's it.

1

Hover. Click. Done.

Activate Hushh with Alt+Shift+H or the toolbar icon. Hover to highlight any element, then click to select it.

We grab the secret.

A DOM observer watches the whole page. Every time that value appears — in any element, anywhere — it gets blurred automatically.

Built to actually work.

  • 🎯

    Follows the value, not the position

    When your secret moves to a new DOM element, the blur follows automatically.

  • 🎥

    Works with any screen recorder

    CSS backdrop-filter renders natively into the OS compositor — Loom, Zoom, Meet, QuickTime all see the blur.

  • 🔒

    Zero data stored or transmitted

    Secrets live in-memory only. No localStorage, no cloud, no account. Cleared on tab close.

  • ⚡

    Engineered for performance

    rAF-batched mutation observer means at most one DOM scan per animation frame. No jank, no lag.

  • ⌨️

    Keyboard shortcut

    Toggle selection mode instantly with Alt+Shift+H. No mouse required to activate.
